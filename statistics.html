The Olympic Games

Statistics

Greatest Olympic Athletes of All Time

Michael Phelps: USA swimming champion with 23 gold medals, 3 silver medals and 2 bronze medals.

Larisa Latynina: Soviet Union artistic gymnast with 9 gold medals, 5 silver medals and 4 bronze medals.

Muhammad Ali: USA boxing legend with 6 gold medals.

Brigit Fischer: German canoe sprint champion with 8 gold medals and 4 silver medals.

Usain Bolt: Jamaican track champion with 8 gold medals and world records in 100 metres, 200 metres and 4x100 metres relay.
