The Olympic Games

Leading international sporting events featuring summer and winter sports competitions in which thousands of athletes from around the world participate in a variety of competitions. With more than 200 nations competing, the Olympic Games are considered the world's foremost sports competition.

A modern take on the ancient Olympic Games held in Olympia, Greece from the 8th century BC, the current Olympic Games have evolved over time to incorporate winter sports, the Paralympic Games, the Youth Olympic Games and more.
